Lockstar Smart Door Lock

Stay secured no matter where you are with our advanced smart door lock system. Designed for modern living, it offers multiple entry methods so you never have to worry about being locked out again.

Multiple Entry Methods for Unmatched Convenience

With Lockstar, you can access your door using your phone's app, a fingerprint scanner, a keypad, a FOB, or even a traditional key. This versatile setup ensures that every person can find the method that best suits their lifestyle and security needs.

Remote Control at Your Fingertips

Lockstar’s user-friendly app allows you to lock or unlock your door remotely with just a few taps. Plus, you can control your door with a long-tap command for added ease, making it perfect for busy individuals and those living away from home.

Always Informed, Always Secure

Enjoy real-time alerts and event history tracking right from your smartphone. Stay updated on who has accessed your door and when, giving you peace of mind and full control over your home security at all times.

Easy Installation, No Hassle

Install your Lockstar Smart Door Lock quickly with just a screwdriver and an optional drill. It’s a straightforward process that doesn’t require complex tools or professional help, saving you time and effort.

Perfect for Rental Properties and More

Lockstar is ideal for landlords and tenants alike. Its remote access features make it easy to manage rental properties and provide secure access to tenants without compromising the safety of the home.

  • Remote control access from your phone or keypad
  • Monitoring features to keep track of every entry
