High-Capacity Paper Towels for Clean, Efficient Use

SofPull Centerpull Paper Towels are designed with efficiency and hygiene in mind, making them the ideal choice for both home and commercial settings. Each roll contains 324 sheets, providing you with a long-lasting supply to handle your daily cleaning needs with ease. Whether you're in a busy kitchen or a high-traffic bathroom, these paper towels offer consistent performance and reliability.

With their white premium quality, these towels maintain a clean appearance and are perfect for use in any environment. The one-hand dispensing feature ensures that you can access a fresh towel without touching the dispenser, helping to reduce germs and cross-contamination. The perforated design allows for effortless, clean tearing without the risk of mishandling or wastage, saving time and effort on every use.

The flexible SofPull Centerpull system ensures smooth and continuous dispensing, keeping your workspace organized and sanitary. Ideal for bulk purchasing, the 6-roll case is both cost-effective and convenient, perfect for households or businesses that rely on a steady supply of clean paper towels.
