Prime-Line U 9590 Door Reinforcement Plate

Upgrade your door's security with this reliable and long-lasting stainless steel lock reinforcement plate. It's the perfect solution for enhancing protection without the need to replace your entire lock system.

Sturdy & Corrosion-Resistant Design

This plate is crafted from high-quality stainless steel, ensuring it remains strong and durable even in harsh weather conditions. Its corrosion-resistant properties make it ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications, providing lasting security and peace of mind.

Easy Installation, No Hassle

Simple to install with all necessary hardware included, this plate is designed for quick and hassle-free setup. Pre-punched mounting holes make the installation process even smoother, allowing you to complete the job in no time.

Versatile Compatibility

Whether you're working with metal, fiberglass, or wooden doors, this reinforcement plate is built to fit securely over a single bore hole. It's engineered for doors with a thickness of 1-3/4 inches and a 2-1/8 inch bore hole, while maintaining a 2-3/8 inch backset from the edge.

Sleek Finish, Modern Aesthetic

The polished stainless steel finish blends seamlessly with your door's design, offering not just protection but also a stylish upgrade. Its low-maintenance nature ensures it stays looking great for years without the need for frequent repairs or replacements.

Cost-Effective Security Solution

This door hardware reinforcement adds significant strength to your existing lock setup. It's a smart, affordable way to enhance your door's security and provide better protection against forced entry without replacing the whole system.

Ideal for Homeowners & DIY Enthusiasts

Perfect for those who want to improve their door security without a full renovation. This stainless steel lock plate is tailored for easy use by homeowners and DIYers, ensuring a secure and stylish upgrade with minimal effort.
